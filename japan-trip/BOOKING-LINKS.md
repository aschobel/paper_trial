## Core Bookings

### 1) teamLab Borderless
- Official: https://www.teamlab.art/e/tokyo/
- Klook: https://www.klook.com/en-US/activity/20707-teamlab-borderless-admission-ticket-tokyo/
- Best target: **Sat Apr 19** or **Sun Apr 20**
- Notes:
  - Klook is often easier when the official site is overloaded
  - Official site currently shows extended closing hours on Apr 16–20

### 2) Spacia X / Tobu Limited Express (Nikko ↔ Tokyo)
- Visitor ticket portal: https://tobu-japantrip-tickets.com/
- Notes:
  - Tickets go on sale at **9:00 AM JST one month before travel**
  - As of today, the Tobu visitor portal is showing **system maintenance**, so live inventory check was blocked
  - Best target: **Wed Apr 23 Nikko → Asakusa**

### 2A) SAPHIR ODORIKO — Special Train Upgrade (Tokyo ↔ Izu)
- Klook guide / booking path: https://www.klook.com/blog/saphir-odoriko-train/
- Schedule/tickets: https://www.klook.com/en-US/japan-rail/saphir-odoriko/
- Notes:
  - This is the **most charming special train** near Tokyo
  - Private compartments available; Premium Green is also great
  - Reservations open **one month before departure**
  - Private compartments often require JR East ticket office / ticket machine booking
  - Best use: swap one Tokyo day for a **Tokyo → Ito / Izu** luxury train day

### 3) Kozantei Ubuya (Kawaguchiko ryokan)
- Official: https://www.ubuya.co.jp/en/
- Backup search: Booking.com / Rakuten Travel / Google Hotels
- Best target: **night of Apr 21**

### 4) Dormy Inn Premium Shibuya-Jingumae
- Official brand site: https://dormy-hotels.com/en/
- Booking.com / Google Hotels also fine
- Nights: **Apr 18, 19, 20, 23**

### 5) International Driving Permit
- AAA branch finder: https://www.aaa.com/office/
- Potrero branch: **2300 16th St, San Francisco**
- Bring: valid license + passport photos + $20
